Places Must Visit

prithvi cafe

I Had Visited In Dec 2018

I was short on time and hence the meet was planned in haste. Had I had more time at hand and planned it well, I would have surely watched a play too. I’ve never seen a theatre act in my life and this could have been the one, unfortunately, it wasn’t. The place is lively during the day but I’m sure it comes alive during the evening. It ticked all the boxes for me – good vibes, good food and pocket friendly. The only thing that didn’t amuse me was the crowd. Being an iconic place, the crowd will always be there. So if you are someone who cannot wait for their food, you might not find Prithvi Cafe that amazing.
